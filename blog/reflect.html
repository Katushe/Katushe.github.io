Building a Website

DBC Phase 0 Week 9

January 6th, 2016

Creating my own website was a great experience. It's so satisfying to know that I've built it up from scratch. In the beginning, the biggest challenge was figuring out how to position elements. It took me a while to really grasp the differences between relative and absolute positioning, as well as the differences between padding and margins. Working on the site definitely helped me learn to distinguish them. Right now, Im going through the process of integrating Bootstrap while still keeping some of my original styling choices. This has been quite the challenge as well, since bootstrap has a lot of defaults I have to override to keep things looking the way I want. But, it has allowed me to make my site responsive, which was my reasoning behind adding Boostrap in the first place. The site is definitely still a work in progress, and I look forward to playing with it more in the coming weeks.
